Estonian Fencing Federation
Estonian [Fencing](2300) Federation (abbreviation EFF; Estonian: Eesti Vehklemisliit) is one of the sport governing bodies in Estonia which deals with fencing.
EFF is established on 2 December 1989 in Tallinn. EFF is a successor of Estonian SSR [Fencing](2300) Federation (Estonian: Eesti NSV Vehklemisf√∂deratsioon) which was established in 1948. EFF is a member of International [Fencing](2300) Federation (FIE).
