Banana custard
[Banana](2900) custard or bananas and custard is a type of traditional pudding made from banana slices mixed with custard.

Preparation
[Banana](2900)s are peeled and then sliced horizontally into thin slices and added to a bowl of custard, the custard is then heated until it is piping hot. The hot custard and banana slices are then served. Some versions are served with chilled custard.
