Albanian Basketball First Division
The [Albania](1534)n [Basketball](2400) First Division is the men's second tier basketball league in [Albania](1534). It is the lower division of the [Albania](1534)n [Basketball](2400) League which is organised by the [Albania](1534)n [Basketball](2400) Association.
