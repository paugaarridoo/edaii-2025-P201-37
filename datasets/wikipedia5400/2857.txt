Fragaria moupinensis
[Fragaria](2850) moupinensis is a species of wild strawberry native to [China](2217). It is similar in appearance to F. nilgerrensis.
All strawberries have a base monoploid count of 7 chromosomes. [Fragaria](2850) moupinensis is tetraploid, having 4 pairs of these chromosomes for a total of 28 chromosomes.
