Bettina Wiesmann
Bettina Margarethe Wiesmann (born 20 October 1966) is a German politician of the Christian Democratic Union (CDU). Born in [Berlin](1387), she has served as a member of the Bundestag from the state of Hesse from 2017 to 2021 and again since 2024.

Political career
Wiesmann became a member of the Bundestag in the [2017 German federal election](1301). In parliament, she was a member of the Committee on Families, Senior Citizens, Women and Youth.
