A2 National Women's Basketball
A2 National Women's [Basketball](2400), also known as A2 Ethniki Women's [Basketball](2400) is the 2nd-tier level women's basketball league in Greece. It is organized by the Hellenic [Basketball](2400) Federation (E.O.K.). Each season, the top two placed teams of the league are promoted to the top-tier level Greek women's division.

History
The A2 National began with the 1997–98 season. The league was split into a two groups format (North-South), starting with the 2003–04 season.
