Fragaria nubicola
[Fragaria](2850) nubicola is a species of wild strawberry native to the Himalayas. It is of no commercial value.
All strawberries have a base haploid count of 7 chromosomes. [Fragaria](2850) nubicola is diploid, having 2 pairs of these chromosomes for a total of 14 chromosomes.
