Bluggoe
Bluggoe, Orinoco, Musa 'Orinoco', or burro is a cultivar of banana.

Genome
Bluggoe is a triploid ABB cultivar.

Cultivation
Bluggoe is a cold hardy banana, growing in USDA zones 8–10 or 7–10.

Tree
10 feet (3.0 metres) to 16 feet (4.9 metres) tall. Width of leaves same dimensions.

Flowers
Pink to cream coloured.

Fruit
About 6 inches (15 cm) long x 2 inches (5 cm) diameter. It is primarily a cooking banana but can be eaten as a dessert banana.
