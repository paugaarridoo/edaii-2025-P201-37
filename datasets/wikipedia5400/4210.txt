Aisinian Aragonese
Aisa [Aragon](2509)ese is a dialect of [Aragon](2509)ese language spoken in Aísa Valley. It is very similar to Aragüés [Aragon](2509)ese and Jaca [Aragon](2509)ese.

Article
The article is like in General [Aragon](2509)ese :o, a, os, as.
As in Somontano de Ayerbe, it is contracted with preposition: d'o, d'a, n'o, n'a, t'o, t'a.
