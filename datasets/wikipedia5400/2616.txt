Batata harra
Batata harra (Arabic: بطاطا حرّة) is a vegetable dish native to Lebanon. It consists of potatoes, red peppers, coriander, chili and garlic that are all fried together in olive oil. They can be served plain or in a pita.

Etymology
Baṯāṯā ḥārrah literally means "spicy potatoes".
