Castellterçol
Castellterçol (Catalan pronunciation: kəsˌteʎtəɾˈsɔl) is a town in [Catalonia](2549). It is in the province of [Barcelona](1000), in the comarca of Moianès; until May 2015 it was in Vallès Oriental. As of 2013, the population was 2,402.
