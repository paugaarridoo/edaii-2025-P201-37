Berkersheim
Berkersheim is a quarter of [Frankfurt](1300) am Main, Germany. It is part of the Ortsbezirk Nord-Ost.
Berkersheim had been an independent town until 1910 when it was suburbanised. It is located in the north-eastern part of [Frankfurt](1300) and borders the districts of Harheim to the north, [Frankfurt](1300)er Berg to the west, Preungesheim and Seckbach to the south and the town of [Bad Vilbel](1357) to the east.
