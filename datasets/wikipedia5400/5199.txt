Azzabas
The Azzabas madhhab is one of the schools of [Islam](5101)ic law in Ibadism. The Ibadi school was founded between the years 1286 and 1386 and is mainly centered in the Maghreb region. In practice, Azzabas promotes a system of collectivism rather than individualism. Decisions earmarked by the Azzabas were conducted within a council, whose members attained their positions via consultations, and its rulings derive from a varied number of sources.
