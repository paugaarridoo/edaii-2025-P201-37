Academia Venezolana de la Lengua
The Academia Venezolana de la Lengua (Spanish for Venezuelan Academy of Language) is an association of academics and experts on Venezuelan Spanish, the variant of the [Spanish language](4301) in Venezuela. It was founded in Caracas on July 26, 1883. It is a member of the [Association of Spanish Language Academies](4346).
