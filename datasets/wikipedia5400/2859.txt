Fragaria nipponica
[Fragaria](2850) nipponica is a species of wild strawberry native to the western side of the Japanese island of HonshÅ«, with a variety [Fragaria](2850) nipponica var. yakusimensis on Yakushima. Some botanists treat it as a synonym of [Fragaria](2850) yezoensis.
All strawberries have a base haploid count of 7 chromosomes. [Fragaria](2850) nipponica is diploid, having 2 pairs of these chromosomes for a total of 14 chromosomes.

[Fragaria](2850) nipponica, particularly var. yakusimensis, is cultivated in Japan for its edible fruit.
