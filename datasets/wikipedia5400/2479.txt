Azerbaijan Basketball League
The [Azerbaijan](1588) [Basketball](2400) League ([Azerbaijan](1588)i: Azərbaycan Basketbol Liqası) is the top men's professional basketball league in [Azerbaijan](1588). The league is governed by the [Azerbaijan](1588) [Basketball](2400) Federation.
