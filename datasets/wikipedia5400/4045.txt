Bruce E. Bursten
Bruce Edward Bursten (March 8, 1954) is an American chemist, professor of chemistry, and was president of the [American Chemical Society](2806). He is provost at Worcester Polytechnic Institute. His research has specialised in inorganic chemistry and metal-containing molecules.

Life
He was born in Chicago, Illinois. He graduated from University of Chicago, and University of Wisconsin-Madison, where he studied with Richard F. Fenske.
He taught at Ohio State University, University of Tennessee, Knoxville, and Barnard College. 
He was AAAS Chemistry Chair and an ACS fellow.
