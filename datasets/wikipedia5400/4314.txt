Academia Guatemalteca de la Lengua
The Academia Guatemalteca de la Lengua (Spanish for Guatemalan Academy of the Language) is an association of academics and experts on the use of the [Spanish language](4301) in Guatemala. It was founded on June 30, 1887. It is a member of the [Association of Spanish Language Academies](4346).
