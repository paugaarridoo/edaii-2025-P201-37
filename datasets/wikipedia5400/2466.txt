Arab Women's Basketball Championship
The Arab Women's [Basketball](2400) Championship (Arabic: البطولة العربية لكرة السلة للسيدات) or simply AWBC is a regional basketball tournament which takes place every two years between women's national teams of the members of the Arab [Basketball](2400) Confederation, representing the Arab world. The first edition was held in 1983 in Jordan.
