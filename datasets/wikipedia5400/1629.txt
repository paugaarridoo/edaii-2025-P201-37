Administrative divisions of Sweden
There are several series of subdivisions of [Sweden](1600).

Judiciary
Supreme Court (Högsta Domstolen), Supreme Administrative Court (Högsta förvaltningsdomstolen)
Courts of appeal (hovrätter), administrative courts of appeal (kammarrätter) (and historically: lagsagor)
District courts (tingsrätter), county administrative courts (länsrätter)
Central executive
Cabinet of [Sweden](1600), Government agencies in [Sweden](1600)
County administrative boards of [Sweden](1600)
Local government
Regions of [Sweden](1600) (regioner) - there are 21
Municipalities of [Sweden](1600) (kommuner) - there are 290
City districts of [Sweden](1600) (stadsdelar or stadsdelsnämndsområden)
Ecclesiastically
Church of [Sweden](1600)
Dioceses (stift)
Kontrakt
Pastorat
Parishes (församlingar)
Historically
Lands of [Sweden](1600)
Provinces of [Sweden](1600)
Hundreds of [Sweden](1600)
Socknar (both parishes and rural municipalities)
Proposed
Regions of [Sweden](1600)
