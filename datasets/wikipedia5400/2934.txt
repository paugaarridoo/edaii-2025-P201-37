Banana Research Station, Kannara
[Banana](2900) Research Station, Kannara is a research Station under the Central Zone of Kerala Agricultural University at Kannara in Thrissur district of Kerala, India.
This research station was established in 1963 to carry out research on bananas and pineapples. The station was brought under the All India Co-ordinated Fruit Improvement Project of the ICAR in 1970. 
When Kerala Agricultural University was established in 1972, [Banana](2900) Research Station, Kannara was taken over by KAU. This station successfully developed tissue culture banana farming in 2011.
