Albanian Basketball Superleague
The [Albania](1534)n [Basketball](2400) Superliga is the men's professional basketball league in [Albania](1534). It was founded in 1946 shortly after the formation of the [Albania](1534)n [Basketball](2400) Association and it is the highest division in the [Albania](1534)n [Basketball](2400) League. The team with the most championships is Partizani who have won 33 championships in total, but none since 1996.
