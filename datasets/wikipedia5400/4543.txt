Bad Brambach
[Bad Brambach](4543) (German: baːt ˈbʁamˌbax ) is a municipality in the Vogtlandkreis district, in Saxony, Germany. It is the southernmost municipality in Saxony and also in what was formerly East Germany. It is a spa town with radon baths.
