Fragaria daltoniana
[Fragaria](2850) daltoniana is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae. It is a strawberry native to the Himalayas. Its fruit has a poor flavor, and is of no commercial value.
All strawberries have a base haploid count of 7 chromosomes. [Fragaria](2850) daltoniana is diploid, having 2 pairs of these chromosomes for a total of 14 chromosomes. [Fragaria](2850) daltoniana is one of the two [Fragaria](2850) species containing a genome that is cold-resistant that can improve the growth and yielding of garden strawberry crops from abiotic stress.
