Bubu Lubu
Bubu Lubu is a popular brand of chocolate bars from Mexico, featuring a strawberry and marshmallow filling with a chocolate covering. Invented in 1978, it is manufactured by Mondelez International via Ricolino. In the [United States](1100), Bubu Lubu are sold in many Mexican grocery stores and international supermarkets. Bubu Lubu is often eaten frozen.
