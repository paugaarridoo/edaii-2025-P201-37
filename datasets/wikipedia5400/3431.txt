Albert Einstein Medal
The [Albert Einstein](3401) Medal is an award presented by the [Albert Einstein](3401) Society in [Bern](3499). First given in 1979, the award is presented to people for "scientific findings, works, or publications related to [Albert Einstein](3401)" each year.
