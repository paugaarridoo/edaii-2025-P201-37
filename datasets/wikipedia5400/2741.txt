Cucumis
Cucumis is a genus of twining, tendril-bearing plants in the family [Cucurbitaceae](2747) which includes the cucumber ([Cucumis sativus](2746)), true melons ([Cucumis melo](2742), including cantaloupe and  honeydew), the horned melon ([Cucumis metuliferus](2744)), and the West [India](2798)n gherkin (Cucumis anguria).
30 species occur in [Africa](1700), and 25 occur in [India](2798), Southeast Asia, and [Australia](1659). However, [Cucumis myriocarpus](2745) was introduced to [Australia](1659) from Sub-Saharan [Africa](1700), and is regarded as an invasive species.
