Brandes House
The Ray Brandes House is a [Frank Lloyd Wright](3201) designed Usonian home located at 2202 212th Avenue SE, Sammamish, Washington It was constructed in 1952. The home is constructed in [Frank Lloyd Wright](3201)'s Usonian style which is designed to create flow between nature, the home and its interior. It is one of the better preserved examples of this style, and one of three homes designed by [Frank Lloyd Wright](3201) in Washington State. Landscaping was selected from local flora and fauna, typical of [Frank Lloyd Wright](3201)'s preference.
