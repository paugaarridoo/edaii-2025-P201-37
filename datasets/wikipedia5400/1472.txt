De Gelderlander
De Gelderlander (founded 1848) is a Dutch daily newspaper focused on Gelderland and immediate surroundings. It is published in [Nijmegen](1400) by the Belgian Persgroep.
De Gelderlander evolved in 1848 from the [Nijmegen](1400) biweekly newspaper De Batavier, published from 1843 to 1845.
