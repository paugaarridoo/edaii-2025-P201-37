Benjamin Beit-Hallahmi
Benjamin Beit-Hallahmi (Hebrew: בנימין בית-הלחמי) (born June 12, 1943) is a professor emeritus of psychology at the University of Haifa, Israel. In 1970 Beit-Hallahmi received a PhD in clinical psychology from Michigan State University.
