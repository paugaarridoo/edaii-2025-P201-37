Anne Quist
Johanne "Anne-Marie" Marie Quist (born 26 December 1957 in [Nijmegen](1400), Gelderland) is a former international rower from the Netherlands, who won the bronze medal in the Women's Eights at the [1984 Summer Olympics](1042) in Los Angeles, California. Her teammates were Marieke van Drogenbroek, Lynda Cornet, Greet Hellemans, Nicolette Hellemans, Harriet van Ettekoven, Martha Laurijsen, Catharina Neelissen, and Wiljon Vaandrager.
