Antonio Trevín
Antonio Ramón María Trevín Lombán (born 27 February 1956) is a Spanish politician and academic. Trevín, a member of the [Spanish Socialist Workers' Party](3937) (PSOE), served as the President of the Principality of [Asturias](3902) from 18 June 1993 to 11 July 1995.
Before being the President of [Asturias](3902), Trevín served as mayor of Llanes from 1987 to 1993. Since 2011, he has served as a member of the Spanish Congress, representing [Asturias](3902).
