25th meridian east
The meridian 25Â° east of Greenwich is a line of longitude that extends from the North Pole across the Arctic Ocean, Europe, [Africa](1530), the Indian Ocean, the Southern Ocean, and [Antarctica](153) to the South Pole.
The 25th meridian east forms a great circle with the 155th meridian west.
Most of the border between Libya and Egypt is defined by the meridian, as is much of the border between Libya and Sudan.
