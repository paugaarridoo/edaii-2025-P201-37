Athlone Stadium
The Athlone Stadium is a stadium in Athlone on the Cape Flats in [Cape Town](1800), South Africa. It is used mostly for soccer matches and it is the home ground of [Cape Town](1800) Spurs. The stadium holds 34,000 people and it was built in 1972.
The stadium was upgraded in the lead up to the [2010 FIFA World Cup](1714) with the intention of using it as a training venue.  The estimated cost of the upgrade was R297 million.
