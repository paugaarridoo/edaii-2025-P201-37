Atlantis Sand Fynbos
Atlantis Sand Fynbos is a critically endangered fynbos vegetation type that occurs to the north of [Cape Town](1800), in the Western Cape, South Africa.
It is listed as critically endangered as it is home to a great many threatened Red List species.
