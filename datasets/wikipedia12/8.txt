British Longhair
The British Longhair, also known as the Highlander or Highland Straight, is a longhaired variant of the British Shorthair [cat](0) [breed](9). Some registries such as [The International Cat Association](1) recognise it as a distinct breed, others such as the [Governing Council of the Cat Fancy](4) treat it as a variant of the British Shorthair, which may be referred to as simply 'British'.

The breed is identical to the British Shorthair aside from its longer and denser coat
