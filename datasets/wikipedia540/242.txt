1979 NBL season
The 1979 NBL season was the inaugural season of the National [Basketball](240) League (NBL). The championship was decided by a sudden death Grand Final between first (St. Kilda Saints) and second (Canberra Cannons).

Teams
Ten teams competed in the 1979 Season.

Regular season
The 1979 regular season took place over 15 rounds between 24 February 1979 and 3 June 1979.
Every team played 18 games, against each opponent twice.
